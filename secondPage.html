Ingredients

  • 300 g (10,5 oz) SAVOIARDI LADYFINGERS
  • 500 g (1,1 lb) of mascarpone cheese cream
  • 4 medium eggs
  • 100 g (3,5 oz) of sugar
  • 300 ml of coffee (better if espresso)
  • 2 tablespoons of rum or MARSALA (optional)
  • unsweetened cocoa powder
